We Are Guided & Inspired By Our Vision

The year 2000, our founder Ajay Agarwal whose vision to create a Place that offered the absolute best selection in Natural stone from all over the world. Over the Past 18 Years, The brand and aspirations have evolved with the goal to be the company that provides the absolute best Natural Stone in the Middle East (Granite, Marble, Onyx, Travertine, Limestone, Sandstone, Slates, Quartz and Porcelain).
We have worked hard to enhance and expand our business. Bringing people and businesses together is what we best.



Our Strategic Focus

Today, Al Asal is a leading natural stone company focusing on Best quality, Fair price, and client satisfaction.
With our focus always to be the market leader and to provide better quality of natural stone, we have our granite processing units in India.
Al Asal has strong tie-ups with top suppliers from Italy, India, Brazil, Spain, Greece, Portugal, Turkey, China, Pakistan, Egypt, and Saudi Arabia and many more.
We also export to Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Bahrain, Oman, Pakistan and Iraq.
